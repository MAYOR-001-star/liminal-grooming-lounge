ABOUT US

The word liminal originates from the Latin word limen, meaning threshold – any point or place of entering or beginning. Liminal Grooming Lounge is a place of transition, growth, and evolution. Liminal is in between what used to be and what is to become. At Liminal Grooming Lounge you become your best present self and can trust in our process, our products, and our individuals. We aim to raise the standards in the men’s hair industry by constantly evolving in our craft through investing in the best quality products, tools, and sanitation practices. We are dedicated, passionate barbers who make a positive impact on each and every client we service. Sit back, trust, transform, and BE LIMINAL
