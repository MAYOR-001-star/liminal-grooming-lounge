SERVICES

At Liminal Grooming Lounge we focus on providing high-quality men’s grooming services by appointment only in a clean, relaxed, and professional enivonment. Our haircuts combine elements of traditional men’s hair styling techniques using scissors with elements of modern barbering using clippers and detailed fading techniques. We differentiate each style by tailoring our haircut service to each individual’s head shape, facial structure, lifestyle, style, and his desired maintenance level for styling. We provide beard trims utilizing professional clippers for trimming the beard, precision free-hand sculpting, and clipper over comb techniques. For line-ups and finishing touches we use professional trimmers, foil-shavers, and a straight razor.
